Status Timeline Component

A responsive tracking component for visualizing service request lifecycles.

Variant: Vertical Service Tracking (Default)

Typical usage in mobile apps or sidebar details.

  1. Request Received

    Oct 24, 09:30 AM

    Your service request #SR-9821 has been received and logged.

  2. Technician Assigned

    Oct 24, 02:15 PM

    Technician Sarah Jenkins has been assigned to your case.

  3. On The Way

    Today, 08:45 AM

    Technician is traveling to your location. Estimated arrival in 15 mins.

  4. Work In Progress

    Service execution and repairs.

  5. Resolved

    Job completion and sign-off.

Used when a workflow is interrupted or cancelled.
